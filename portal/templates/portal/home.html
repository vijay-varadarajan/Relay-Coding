About

Ideate and implement an innovative solution to a problem statement! Experience the thrill of playing fun and exciting side games to earn points!


Relay Coding is a high-octane 36-hour technical Hackathon unlike anything seen before. It is a showcase of innovation where participants will be in a race against time to build pioneering projects aimed at solving real-world problems.

The trademark of this event will be the relay format side challenges. These challenges inject an extra layer of excitement, allowing teams to enhance their prospects and add a twist to the competition.

The initial phase of the event consists of ideation, project building, and relay challenges. The projects will then be reviewed and shortlisted teams will proceed to the final phase.

In the final phase, the teams will be presenting and pitching their projects to a panel of judges. This final sprint is where innovation converges with strategy. Teams will get to showcase their technical acumen along with their ability to present their ideas. All the teams will have only one goal, securing a coveted podium finish.
